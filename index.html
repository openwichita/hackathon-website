Open Wichita Hackathon ICT at Riverfest

June 4 & 5, 2016

We live in a world that is constantly being transformed by new technology and ideas. The resources are available to anyone who decides that they want to create something and see what comes of it. Tools such as the internet, social media, coding and all the data we're collecting open up incredible possibilities.

Why not use these tools to transform the city around us? Our local community is ripe for innovation, and anyone could have an idea that makes a huge impact. Couple that with a group of like minded individuals to help each other along in their efforts and big things start to happen.

This is what Open Wichita exists to do. It is a civic hacking community working on projects to make Wichita better through the use of technology. Other cities all around the world are already benefiting wildly from the effects of these efforts, why not here?.

Who?

Do you have ideas that could innovate and improve the community? Do you enjoy technology and creating things? Does it sounds exciting to be a part of a team that makes a positive impact on the world around you? If you answered yes to any of these questions, this event is for you.

What?

A civic hackathon is an event where various teams and individuals generate ideas and create things that make their community a better place. Bring your various skills to form teams that can execute ideas and see what change can be made.

When?

Teams will be able to work from noon on Saturday, June 4 until noon the next day. There will be some introductory and preparatory remarks before the kickoff starting at 11:00 AM on the 4th. After work ends there will be presentations from which judges will choose a winner.

Where?

Riverfest 2016! You must have a Riverfest button to attend. The Hackathon itself will be taking place in the TBD. The event space will have plenty of table space for teams as well as amenities such as a rest area and small concessions.

Why?

Wichita is in a state of flux right now. Civic pride is on the rise, technology based companies are settling in, developer communities are growing and anyone can put forth effort to make a real impact. Technology makes it all the more possible. Besides, it's fun to build things.

How?

Register below to get signed up! Then spend some time thinking of your favorite idea to innovate in the community. Tell everyone you know about it and get them involved. Then show up on June 4th and get ready to see what you're capable of in a 24 hour period!

Event Schedule

Event Schedule

Here's what we're going to do before and during Hackathon ICT:

Invite any person who wants to be involved to a 24 hour Hackathon taking place on June 4-5 during Riverfest.

Tell people to bring their best ideas, or just show up to form and team and work with others.

Spend the entire 24 hour period making these ideas a reality by accomplishing as much as you can on the efforts.

Have everyone present what they accomplished and pick some winners based on achievement, ambition, creativity, and value.

Keep the movement going forward through continued involvement with the Open Wichita community and the development of the projects.

It's incredible what can be accomplished by small teams of people these days. Let's get everyone together with one single purpose: **make the community better**, and see what kind of impact can be had.
